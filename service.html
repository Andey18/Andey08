Services:


Health Services:

Barangay San Nicolas offers essential health services, including regular medical check-ups, immunization programs for children, and maternal and child health services. Health education workshops and health fairs are also conducted to promote community well-being.


Community Development Programs:

The barangay focuses on sustainable development through infrastructure improvements and livelihood programs. These initiatives provide training in skills development and entrepreneurship, empowering residents and supporting local businesses.


Youth and Sports Development:

To engage the youth, Barangay San Nicolas provides sports leagues, educational workshops, and mentorship opportunities. These programs aim to develop leadership skills and foster community involvement among young people.


Disaster Preparedness and Response:

The barangay prioritizes safety through disaster preparedness training and regular drills. In emergencies, it ensures swift assistance and resources for affected families, promoting a well-organized response to natural calamities.

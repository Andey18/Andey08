Let me Introduce our Brgy. San Nicolas!

Barangay San Nicolas is one of the barangays in San Pablo City, Laguna, in the Philippines. Its history is closely tied to the larger history of San Pablo City, one of the oldest settlements in Laguna.


With a population of about 7,255 as of the 2020 Census, Barangay San Nicolas represents 2.54% of San Pablo City's total population. The barangay has experienced steady growth and significant demographic changes since the 1990s.


Our committed team of officials is focused on community development through infrastructure projects, youth engagement, and support for local businesses. We prioritize education, healthcare, and disaster preparedness to enhance the quality of life for residents.


Barangay San Nicolas offers a vibrant community rich in culture and convenience, strategically located near essential amenities. With strong community engagement and a focus on youth involvement, it provides a welcoming atmosphere where tradition meets modern living.
